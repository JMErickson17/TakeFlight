Description
Search, save and compare thousands of flights right from within the app. TakeFlight allows you to explore new and exciting locations as well as save flights that you aren't ready to book yet. Once you are ready to book, TakeFlight offers all of the information you need to find the flight on the carrier's website or through your preferred booking outlet. In-app flight booking along with many more features are coming soon!
